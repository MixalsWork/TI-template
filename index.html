What you need for tech
EXTERNAL TRAINING

The vehicle must comply with all entries in the registration documents for it.

The vehicle must be clean, both outside and inside.

Windshield without cracks.

The vehicle must be filled with all necessary operating fluids.

INTERNAL PREPARATION

The brake system and handbrake must operate without failure. The tires must be the same size and pattern on the same axle as the corresponding treads.

VEHICLE DOCUMENTS

Certificate of state registration of the vehicle.

CLIENT DOCUMENTS

Identity document of the driver.

Requirements for cars for inspection
Before arriving at the test laboratory, the driver and / or car owner must check:
  1. Serviceability of external lighting devices, alarm systems
  2. Serviceability of brake system
  3. Condition of steering elements and chassis
  4. Uniformity of wear of tires and residual height of a protector
  5. Integrity and tightness of technical fluid tanks
  6. Integrity of glass, lack of additional cover on it
  7. Serviceability of windshield washers and wipers
  8. Equipped with fire extinguisher, first aid kit, spare wheel and emergency stop sign
  9. We also remind you that the car must be free of dirt, corrosion and filled with all fluids in accordance with the operating instructions

What does mandatory mean?
Technical inspection of the vehicle involves checking the technical condition of the vehicle, namely:
  1. - brake and steering systems;
  2. - external lighting fixtures;
  3. - pneumatic tires and wheels;
  4. - light transmission of glass;
  5. - gas cylinder equipment (if available);
  6. - other elements in the part directly related to road safety and environmental protection;
  7. - photofixation of the inspection process
